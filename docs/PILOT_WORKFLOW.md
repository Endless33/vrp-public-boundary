# Pilot Workflow

## Overview

Every VRP Pilot follows a structured engineering process.

The objective is to validate interoperability, deployment readiness and observable runtime behavior under real operating conditions.

Each Pilot is planned individually.

---

## Phase 1

Technical Introduction

Objectives

- understand the environment
- identify engineering goals
- define Pilot scope

---

## Phase 2

Environment Review

Typical activities

- network topology review
- deployment planning
- compatibility assessment
- infrastructure discussion

---

## Phase 3

Pilot Preparation

Typical activities

- deployment planning
- runtime configuration
- validation planning
- evidence strategy

---

## Phase 4

Pilot Execution

Possible activities

- deployment
- migration validation
- recovery validation
- interoperability testing
- evidence generation

---

## Phase 5

Engineering Review

Pilot outcomes may include

- engineering observations
- validation summary
- evidence review
- deployment recommendations

---

## Phase 6

Commercial Evaluation

Organizations may continue with

- extended Pilot
- production planning
- commercial deployment
- long-term partnership

---

## Engineering Principle

Every Pilot is an engineering engagement.

It is not a marketing demonstration.
