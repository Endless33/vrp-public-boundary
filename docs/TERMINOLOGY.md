# Terminology

## Protected Runtime

The licensed implementation responsible for runtime execution.

---

## Public Boundary

The engineering layer available for documentation and integration.

---

## Evidence

Observable data exported for external verification.

---

## Verdict

A deterministic public outcome produced by the runtime.

---

## Pilot

A controlled commercial evaluation of VRP.

---

## Contract

A public interface describing interaction without exposing implementation.

---

## Schema

A versioned structure describing public data.
